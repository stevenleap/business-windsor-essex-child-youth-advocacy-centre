About the Centre

Windsor Essex Child/Youth Advocacy Centre (WECYAC) provides a safe, child-friendly location for children and youth to disclose their abuse to a specially trained team of professionals. We are committed to raising public awareness about the existence of child and youth abuse in our community, and to fostering a supportive environment for healing.

Serving Windsor and Essex County, Ontario.

Program Pathways

Four coordinated support paths, each designed to meet children and families where they are.

Child & Youth Advocacy

A coordinated team of professionals provides a safe space for children and youth to disclose abuse and receive support.

Why Families Turn to Us

Specially trained team

Every professional works with a deep understanding of child development and trauma-informed care.

Safe, child-friendly environment

Our space is designed to feel warm, secure, and welcoming — never clinical or intimidating.

Community-focused awareness

We work directly with local partners to build a network of prevention and support across Windsor & Essex County.
