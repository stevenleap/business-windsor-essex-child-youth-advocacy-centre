Our Purpose

Windsor Essex Child/Youth Advocacy Centre (WECYAC) provides a safe location for children and youth to disclose their abuse to a specially trained team of professionals. Located in Windsor, Ontario, the centre is committed to raising public awareness regarding the existence of child/youth abuse in our community.

“A safe, coordinated space for children and youth to disclose abuse, and a community committed to awareness.”
  • Specially trained team of professionals
  • Safe, child-friendly environment
  • Coordinated multi-agency approach
  • Community-focused awareness efforts
